About Me

What I've been up to

Over the last 25 years, I've been lucky enough to build up a successful and well respected floristry business in Cambridge. From humble beginnings in my garden workshop to running my own dedicated shop, my floristry journey has seen me design and create floral arrangements for all manner of occassions and clients including award winning flowers at Chelsea and a bouquet for Her Majesty the Queen.


Having dedicated the last two decades to retail floral design, it's now time for me to engage my love for demonstrating and tutoring, and to make use of my floristry teaching qualifications to help others ignite their passion for flowers.
